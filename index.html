acktoberfest 2021...a tribute

Hacktoberfest, now in its 8th year, is a month-long celebration of open source software run by DigitalOcean.

During the month of October, they invite us to join other open-source software enthusiasts, beginners, and the developer community by contributing to open-source projects.

However, if you are an aspiring developer like me battling imposter syndrome and find it difficult to find a project to contribute to then here we are with the perfect place to do that intimidating first pull-request or polish our git-commit technique.

So without any further ado, here are the tributes to Hacktoberfest 2021 from the newbie and beginner developers out there...
